ToC Committees

Accounting, Fundraising, & Sponsorship

*2015 Goals: Raise $9000 for Project C.O.D.E
  • Maintain ToC Overall Budget and Accounting
  • Work with all teams to understand financial/budget requirements
  • Define fundraising goals based on upcoming events/classes
  • Develop and execute fundraising initiatives
  • Create and distribute annual sponsorship packages for individual and businesses/corporations
  • Coordinate with CONNECT team to identify and manage sponsoring ToC Partners

Technology, Communications & Media

*2015 Goals: Create Slack Channel and ToC Newsletter
  • Develop, enhance and maintain ToC website and Connect Platform (future).
  • Shoot, edit and publish photography/videography.
  • Work with events team to coordinate with event photography/videography and communication requirements.
  • Manage social media accounts and communications
  • Set event management goals for the year and present to leadership for approval

Events Management

*2015 Goals: Streamline Event Check-in process & Create Event Surveys
  • Work with Education and Connect teams to design event schedule, requirements(refreshments/materials/equipments) and devise budget.
  • Meet with Fundraising/Sponsorship lead to obtain sponsors for event as needed.
  • Coordinate with TCM team for event communications, photography or videography requirements.
  • Scout and secure location and meet with event vendors (if required).
  • Manage eventbrite account for events.
  • Secure event badges/swag, coordinate day of event activities including setup, guest check in, and breakdown, and facilitate post event surveys.
  • Report to leadership post event feedback.
  • Set event management goals for the year and present to leadership for approval

Technology Education (Project CODE)

*2015 Goals: Reach 120 children through education.
  • Develop/Review technology education for K-12
  • Develop/Review technology education for Adults (Non-professional)
  • Develop class plan including course goals/objectives, intended audience ages and size and equipment/budget needs
  • Coordinate with event team to secure classroom locations/equipment and obtain volunteers through CONNECT
  • Train ToC educators, teaching/classroom assistants
  • Set education delivery goals for the year and present to leadership for approval
